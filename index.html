Questions

Is this bots?

No. We never touch your song on streaming platforms, we don't add it to playlists, and we don't link it anywhere. We post your song on our music pages, and real people hear it and go look it up themselves. That's why the saves and streams you see are real.

How long does a campaign take?

A 5,000 campaign is usually finished within a day or two. Larger campaigns take a few days more.

What do you need from me?

Just your artist name, the song title, and optionally a timestamp if you want a specific part of the track featured. You give us all of it at checkout.

Where is the audience?

Almost all of our audience is in the US. The best way to see the impact is to open Spotify for Artists and filter your listeners and saves by country.

Can you guarantee streams or saves?

No, and nobody honestly can. We guarantee the reach: the number of real people who hear your song. Whether they love it and go stream it is up to the song.

What's your refund policy?

If we don't deliver the reach you paid for, you get a full refund. Email us and we'll return your payment. Once a campaign has fully delivered, that run is complete and non-refundable.
