FAQ

Questions artists ask us

Is this bots or fake views?

No. We manage a network of music-themed TikTok accounts with real, organically-built audiences. Your song is featured in UGC-style posts on those accounts, and the views come from TikTok's organic reach. We send you links to every post, and you'll see the proof in your own stats, with saves and streams coming from the US.

What do you need from me for the free trial?

Just a link to your song and an email address so we can send you the results. Your sound needs to be at least 9 seconds long. That's the only requirement. No payment details, no contracts, no access to your accounts.

Do you guarantee my song will blow up?

We'll be straight with you: nobody can guarantee people will like your song, and anyone who says otherwise is selling you something fake. What we can guarantee is that people will see it and hear it: real US TikTok users, on real posts. If the song connects, you'll see it in your saves and streams.

What's the catch with the free trial?

There isn't one. This method is new and we know the results sound too good to be true, so we'd rather prove it than pitch it. If the trial works for your song, we think you'll want a bigger campaign. That's the whole bet.

How long does a campaign take?

It depends on the size. Smaller campaigns (anything under 10,000 views, including the free trial) deliver results in a maximum of 2 days. Larger campaigns take a few days more.

Will I see the posts?

Yes. You receive a link to every post made with your song, so you can watch them perform for yourself. Meanwhile, check your own dashboard: you'll see saves and streams coming in from the United States.

What kind of music works best?

We work with independent artists across genres. If your song has a strong hook or a moment that works in short-form video, it's a good fit, and the free trial is exactly how we find out together.
